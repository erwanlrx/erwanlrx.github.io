About

Artificial Intelligence (AI) will impact the future world's shape. While studying AI, I realized how narrow was my current understanding of its potential applications. Above all, I felt that, despite its hype and strength, most AI applications were completely disconnected from concrete world issues, and so was I. This awareness motivated the reconnaiction blog where posts are either in French or in English. As a young researcher, focused on climate extremes' evolution, I still aim to reconnect AI/Statistics with life-saving problems. However, I do not believe that the change of paradigm, raised by climate change, can only be tackled scientifically. Thus, this blog contains both scientific popularization and poetic life-interrogations of varying quality mostly inspired by books I recently read.

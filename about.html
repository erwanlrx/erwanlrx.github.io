About

Artificial Intelligence (AI) will impact the future world's shape. While studying deep learning, I realized how narrow was my current understanding of its potential applications. Above all, I felt that, despite its hype and strength, most AI applications were completely disconnected from concrete world issues, and so was I.

This paradox motivated the reconnaiction blog where posts are either in French or in English.

Even if as a Phd student, strugling on climate extremes statistical modeling, I still aim to reconnect AI with life-saving problems. However, since its inception, this blog's focus has drifted away and is now filled with crazy/bad poetic life-interrogations mostly inspired from books I recently read.

About

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is crucial. Its impact (scientifically, socially, philosophically) is shaping the future world. While studying deep learning for computer vision, I realized how limited is my understanding of AI implications. This paradox motivated the reconnaiction blog. My initial modest project was to find some time to share quick summaries of AI advances.

My name is Erwan Le Roux. I am computer vision and machine learning researcher. I started this blog at the end of my master thesis with Gregory Rogez and Cordelia Schmid in THOTH team at INRIA Grenoble.

Links

Then main recent results, such as LSTM or attention mechanisms, are well illustrated in Colah's blog and Distill To get thorough explanations, go for the Deep Learning book (2016) by Goodfellow, Bengio and Courville.
